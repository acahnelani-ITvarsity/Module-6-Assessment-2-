The Polo Vivo 1.4 Comfortline is a motor vehicle from Volkswagen, with front wheel drive, a front mounted engine and a 5 door hatchback body style. Power is produced by a double overhead camshaft, 1.4 litre naturally aspirated 4 cylinder engine, with 4 valves per cylinder that provides power and torque figures of 84 bhp (85 PS/63 kW) at 5000 rpm and 132 N·m (97 lb·ft/13.5 kgm) at 3750 rpm respectively. The power is delivered to the wheels via a 5 speed manual gearbox.
